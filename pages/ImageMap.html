Image Map

This is a watercolour painting of mine to record Venice scene. Hope Italy gets well this year.
I have set 5 image maps links in my painting. You can try to click the canal water, the Venice tourists, the sky, the Italian people who is paddling the boat, the Venice boat which is called Gondola and the Venice main building.
There is a label shown for each image map when hover.
Also, it will give hints with a written javascript alert window to tell you if you click the right stuff , and after that it will jump to different webpages with introductions of these elements in Venice.
