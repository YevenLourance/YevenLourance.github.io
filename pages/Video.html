This is a video videoed by myself to show you how I create the paintings and sketching in the last year. I video by my phone on a small holder. I also proceed this video with iMovie. I share my timelines of my art and materials as this video shown. I show you 5 different parts with captions, transitions and titles created by myself:
  1. The watercolour materials for watercolour painting: I use 2 watercolour paintings sets
  2. Colour pencils sets for colour pencil drawing: I use 2 different colour pencil drawings
  3. Sketching sets for sketching: I have one set of sketching set with charcoal pencils
  4. The watercolour pads with my own paintings: I have 2 watercolour pads, one big and one small
  5. The sketching pads with my own sketching: I have 2 sketching pads, one big and one small
